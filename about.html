About Me

My journey as an English language teacher began in 2015 when I started working as a language assistant at a school in Madrid, Spain. After completeing my TEFL qualification at the University of Catalonia, I moved to Chile and started teaching English to training Navy cadets. It was at this time that I began creating online content for my instagram channel @englishidiomswithizz, which is aimed at intermediate-advanced students who want to learn everday english idioms.
